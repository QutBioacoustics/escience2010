Registration

Online registration has now closed. The registration desk will be open tomorrow from 8am where onsite registration can be completed.

Invitation Letter

On advice from the Australian Department of Immigration, invitation letters are not needed as part of your visa application to visit Australia.

Instead, it is recommended a copy of your registration confirmation letter should be provided. Once you complete your registration, an electronic copy of this confirmation will be sent to the email address you provided.

This confirmation letter should then be provided with your visa application. For further information please visit the Department's website.
