Conditions

If you wish to book rooms for dates which are not available via this system, please book the dates that are available and then email the event managers with the additional nights you require.

Due to hotel requirements, all bookings must be made by 4 November 2010. After this date new bookings will need to be made directly with the hotel and the rates quoted below cannot be guranteed. Please book early to avoid disappointment. We will still make changes to existing bookings, if sent to the Event Managers in writing.

Please note:
In order to secure your accommodation a credit card number must be supplied at the time of booking. This card will not be charged by the event managers, or by the hotel - it is for a guarantee only.

Upon check-in if you are the cardholder you may use this card to pay the full balance. If you are not the card holder you will need to either have a letter of authority authorising the hotel to charge the card, or supply your own card.

If you wish to pre-pay your accommodation you will need to contact the hotel three weeks out from the conference and arrange this with them. Please note if you contact the hotel any earlier your name may not appear in their booking system.

Invoices/receipts for accommodation expenses can ONLY be issued by the hotel directly.

  

Conditions

Seven days before arrival the full amount of the total accommodation cost will be charged.
